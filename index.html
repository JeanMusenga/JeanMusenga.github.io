MUSENGAMANA JEAN DE DIEU

I am a Ph.D. student (2019-) in Computer Science at Wuhan University, advised by Prof. Liang Peng. My research focuses on Empirical Software Engineering, Software Architecture, Mining Software Repositories, and Natural Language Processing (NLP), with a particular interest in Large Language Models (LLMs). I explore mining architectural knowledge from software artifacts like code, text, and images.
