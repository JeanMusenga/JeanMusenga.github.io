About Me

I am a Ph.D. student (2019-) in Computer Science at Wuhan University, advised by Prof. Liang Peng. My research focuses on Empirical Software Engineering, Software Architecture, Mining Software Repositories, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and I am currently interested in Large Language Models (LLMs). Specifically, I explore areas such as mining architectural knowledge from software artifacts such as code, text, images, etc.
